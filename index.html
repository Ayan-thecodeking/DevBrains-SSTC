about DevBrains

DevBrains is a Community for everyone

DevBrains is a student-driven club from Shri Shankaracharya Technical Campus, We focus on new-age technologies and open-source principles.

The club aims to provide a platform for students to explore and learn about the latest developments in the field of computer science

The club conducts workshops, seminars, and competitions to help students develop their technical skills and stay up-to-date with the latest technologies. The club also provides opportunities for students to work on projects and collaborate with their peers. The club is open to all students, regardless of their major or level of experience.
